Website Classifications

Explore the different types of websites and web technologies as taught in KIIT Polytechnic by Abhaya Kumar Panda.

Static Websites

Static websites are the simplest kind of website and are made up of web pages with fixed content. Each page is coded in HTML and displays the same information to every visitor.

Dynamic Websites

Dynamic websites contain web pages that are generated in real-time. These websites often access information from a database or content management system.

Web Portals

Web portals serve as a gateway to a variety of information, resources, and services. They are often used by large organizations to consolidate access to tools and information.

Social Networking Sites (SNS)

Social networking sites allow users to create profiles, interact with others, and share information across a virtual network.

RSS Feeds

RSS stands for Really Simple Syndication or Rich Site Summary and is a way for users to stay updated with their favorite websites without having to visit them manually.
